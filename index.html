Mosaic

One word a day, found.

A single curated word each day — its definition, its etymology, and a short original passage where the same word is hidden for you to find. Build a streak, browse the archive, keep your favorites. Fully offline, on iPhone.

What it does

01

One word, every day

A daily word with a factual definition, its etymology, and an example sentence. The same word appears on every device, generated from the calendar day — no account required to begin, no server, fully offline.

02

Find it in the passage

Each day comes with a short, original passage where the word is hidden in tappable text. Tap the right word to solve the challenge and earn the day.

03

Streaks, archive & favorites

Keep a daily streak alive, revisit past words in the archive, and star the ones you love. Your stats are always derived live from the words you have actually seen and found.

04

Yours, and private

Everything lives on your device and syncs through your own iCloud. Sign in with Apple is optional, there is no tracking, and the app asks for zero device permissions.
