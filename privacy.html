Mosaic

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Mosaic (“the app,” “we,” “our”) is built to be private by design. The app runs entirely on your device and uses only your own iCloud account through Apple’s CloudKit service. We do not operate our own servers, we do not run any tracking, and we do not embed third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. This policy explains exactly what that means.

1. Who we are

Mosaic is an independent iOS app. For any privacy question or request, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com. The developer acts as the data controller for the limited personal data described below.

2. Our core principle: on-device and your own iCloud

Everything you do in Mosaic — the words you have seen, the find-it challenges you have solved, your streak, and your favorites — is stored locally on your device. If you are signed in to iCloud, this data may be mirrored to the private database of your own iCloud account using Apple’s CloudKit, so it can sync across your devices. That data lives in your Apple account, governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy. We cannot read the contents of your private iCloud database. If you are not signed in to iCloud, the app simply works in local-only mode.

3. Data we process

  • App content you generate: your visited words, solved challenges, streak history, and favorites. Stored on-device and, optionally, in your private iCloud database.
  • Sign in with Apple (optional): if you choose to sign in, Apple provides a stable, app-specific identifier so the app can recognize you. You may use Apple’s “Hide My Email” feature. Sign in with Apple is entirely optional; the app is fully usable without it. We do not handle or store any password.
  • Purchase status: whether you have unlocked Mosaic Pro is determined live by Apple’s StoreKit from your App Store entitlements. We do not store your payment details — Apple processes all payments. A minimal, non-identifying paid-status record may be written to a CloudKit record so the developer can see aggregate paid status; it does not contain your name, email, or app content.
  • Required-reason system APIs: the app uses a small set of Apple-declared APIs (such as on-device user defaults, file timestamps, system boot time, and available disk space) solely for the app to function correctly. These are not used for tracking and are declared in the app’s privacy manifest.

4. Data we do not collect

  • No advertising identifiers and no cross-app or cross-site tracking.
  • No third-party analytics, advertising, or marketing SDKs.
  • No access to your camera, photos, microphone, location, contacts, health, motion, or files — the app requests zero device permissions.
  • No selling or sharing of personal information with third parties for their own purposes.

5. How we use data

The limited data above is used only to make the app work: to show you today’s word, to maintain your streak and stats, to remember your favorites, to sync across your own devices via iCloud, and to honor your one-time Pro purchase. We do not use your data to build advertising profiles.

6. Legal bases (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Where the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation applies, we process data on these bases: contract (to provide the app’s core features you requested), legitimate interests (to keep the app working reliably and to understand aggregate paid status), and consent (for optional Sign in with Apple, which you may decline). You may withdraw consent at any time by signing out.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to access, correct, export, restrict, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. Because most data lives on your device and in your own iCloud account, you are in direct control:

  • Delete in-app: use Settings → Delete Account to remove your local data and disconnect your identity.
  • Delete everything: delete the app to remove its on-device data; you can also remove its data from your iCloud account in iOS Settings.
  • Requests: email s0533495227@gmail.com and we will respond within the time required by law.

California (CCPA/CPRA): we do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined, and we do not discriminate against you for exercising your rights. You may exercise your rights using the same contact above.

8. Data retention

On-device and iCloud data is retained until you delete it, delete your account in-app, or remove the app. The minimal paid-status record is retained only as long as needed to reflect entitlement status.

9. Children’s privacy

Mosaic is rated 4+ and is intended for a general audience. It is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app contains only original, family-friendly content. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International transfers

Any syncing is handled by Apple’s iCloud infrastructure. Where data is transferred internationally, it is handled under Apple’s safeguards described in Apple’s privacy documentation. We do not transfer your app content to any other servers.

11. Security

Data on your device is protected by iOS, and iCloud data is protected by your Apple account’s security. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but because we do not run our own servers, your app content is never exposed to us.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact

Questions or requests: s0533495227@gmail.com.
