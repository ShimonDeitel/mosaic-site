Mosaic

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Mosaic (“the app,” “we,” “our”) is built to be private by design. The app runs entirely on your device. There is no account and no sign-in, we do not use iCloud or any cloud sync, we do not operate our own servers, we do not run any tracking, and we do not embed third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. We collect no personal data. This policy explains exactly what that means.

1. Who we are

Mosaic is an independent iOS app. For any privacy question or request, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com. Because the app collects no personal data, there is no personal data for the developer to hold about you.

2. Our core principle: everything stays on your device

Everything you do in Mosaic — the words you have seen, the find-it challenges you have solved, your streak, and your favorites — is stored locally on your device and nowhere else. There is no account, no sign-in, and no iCloud or cloud sync. Your data never leaves your device, is never sent to us, and is never mirrored to any server. If you use Mosaic on more than one device, each device keeps its own separate, local progress.

3. Data we process

  • App content you generate: your visited words, solved challenges, streak history, and favorites. Stored only on your device. We never receive a copy.
  • Purchase status: whether you have unlocked Mosaic Pro is determined live by Apple’s StoreKit from your App Store entitlements. We do not store your payment details — Apple processes all payments. No purchase record is sent to us or to any server we operate.
  • Required-reason system APIs: the app uses a small set of Apple-declared APIs (such as on-device user defaults, file timestamps, system boot time, and available disk space) solely for the app to function correctly. These are not used for tracking and are declared in the app’s privacy manifest.

4. Data we do not collect

  • No advertising identifiers and no cross-app or cross-site tracking.
  • No third-party analytics, advertising, or marketing SDKs.
  • No access to your camera, photos, microphone, location, contacts, health, motion, or files — the app requests zero device permissions.
  • No selling or sharing of personal information with third parties for their own purposes.

5. How we use data

The on-device content above is used only to make the app work on your device: to show you today’s word, to maintain your streak and stats, to remember your favorites, and to honor your one-time Pro purchase. None of it is sent to us, and we do not use your data to build advertising profiles.

6. Legal bases (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Because Mosaic collects no personal data and processes everything locally on your device, in most cases there is no personal-data processing by us to which the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation would apply. To the extent any provision of the app’s core features is treated as processing, the legal basis is contract (to provide the features you requested). We do not rely on your data for advertising or profiling.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to access, correct, export, restrict, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. Because all of your data lives only on your device, you are in direct control:

  • Delete in-app: use the reset options in Settings to clear your local progress.
  • Delete everything: delete the app to remove all of its on-device data. Because nothing is stored off your device, that fully removes your data.
  • Requests: email s0533495227@gmail.com and we will respond within the time required by law.

California (CCPA/CPRA): we do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined, and we do not discriminate against you for exercising your rights. You may exercise your rights using the same contact above.

8. Data retention

On-device data is retained until you clear it in the app or remove the app. Because nothing is stored off your device, deleting the app deletes everything.

9. Children’s privacy

Mosaic is rated 4+ and is intended for a general audience. It is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app contains only original, family-friendly content. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International transfers

Your app content never leaves your device, so there are no international data transfers. We do not transfer your app content to iCloud or to any other servers.

11. Security

Data on your device is protected by iOS device security. No method of storage is completely secure, but because your app content stays on your device and we do not run our own servers, your app content is never exposed to us.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact

Questions or requests: s0533495227@gmail.com.
